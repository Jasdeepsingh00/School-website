STUDENTS

Baba Dharam Singh Academy continues to academically and spiritually develop each student of our faith-based community. In such an atmosphere all students, faculty, and members of the school community build a loving relationship with God and a deep understanding and knowledge of God within the Catholic faith. A student growing in a loving, learning, and worshiping community is given the best opportunity to integrate faith with culture in a contemporary view of the world.
Baba Dharam Singh Academy strives to create an atmosphere conducive to learning by helping our students acquire the necessary skills and knowledge needed for their future educational career goals, in addition to functioning in society with lifelong success.
Baba Dharam singh Academy recognizes all parent/guardians as "partners" towards the development of our students' Catholic education and faith.

PARENTS

In a day, a child spends one fourth of his time in school and three fourths of his time with his parents. Understanding this fact, parents should never think that by sending their ward to a good school, they have got rid of their responsibilities. The parents and the teachers work in partnership for the all-round development of a child. It is important to teach students to respect school discipline and be proud of it.

ADMISSIONS

Admissions Rules:
1. The school session is from April to March.
2. Admission test is compulsory for all candidates seeking fresh admission to school. No candidate will be admitted unless he/she has appeared in the test and qualified for the class to which admission is sought.
School Rules:
1. Latest comers will not be allowed to enter the school and will be sent home at the risk of parents/guardians.
2. The school will not take any responsibility in such cases. Every student is expected to attend the school regularly and on time.
